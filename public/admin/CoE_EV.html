Aim and Objectives:

The Centre of Excellence (CoE) in Electric Vehicles at Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar aims to drive innovation and research in sustainable transportation technologies. With the global shift towards eco-friendly mobility, this CoE focuses on developing advanced electric vehicle systems, including charging infrastructure development and troubleshoot, battery technologies, power electronics, and smart charging infrastructure. It strives to equip students and researchers with cutting-edge skills to address the challenges of energy efficiency, performance optimization, and environmental impact. The CoE also aims to collaborate with industry partners to foster practical solutions and accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles.

The Key Objectives of Center of Excellence (CoE):


1. Innovation and Research in Sustainable Transportation

The CoE focuses on advancing cutting-edge research to develop sustainable and eco-friendly transportation technologies. It aims to create innovative solutions that reduce dependence on fossil fuels and lower carbon emissions.

2. Development of Advanced Electric Vehicle Systems

The center works on enhancing core EV components such as battery technologies, power electronics, and charging infrastructure. This includes improving efficiency, durability, and integration of these systems.

3. Skill Development for Energy Efficiency and Performance

The CoE provides specialized training and hands-on experience to students and researchers. The goal is to build expertise in optimizing energy usage and vehicle performance for next-generation electric vehicles.

4. Addressing Environmental Impact

The center aims to design and promote electric vehicle technologies that minimize environmental harm. This includes efforts to reduce pollutants and promote sustainable manufacturing and disposal practices.

5. Industry Collaboration for Practical Solutions

The CoE partners with industry leaders to translate research into real-world applications. These collaborations help accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles by addressing market needs and technological challenges.
