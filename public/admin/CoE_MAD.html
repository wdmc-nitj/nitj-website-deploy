Aim and Objectives:


The Center of Excellence (CoE) for Mobile Application Development at Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar is dedicated to advancing innovation, skill development and research in the rapidly growing field of mobile technologies. With smartphones becoming the backbone of digital transformation, this CoE focuses on nurturing professionals who can design, develop and deploy scalable, secure and user friendly mobile applications to meet real world demands.


Key Objectives:


  1. Skill Development and Training: Providing structured training for students, professionals and minority community members in mobile app development across Android, iOS, and cross-platform technologies.
  2. Research and Development: Establishing an ecosystem for research scholars and postgraduate students to create cost effective, high performance mobile solutions addressing societal and industrial challenges.
  3. Industry Collaboration: Acting as an incubation and consultancy hub for startups, entrepreneurs, and MSMEs to prototype, test and scale their mobile app ideas.
  4. Knowledge Sharing: Engaging students, faculty and the wider community through workshops, seminars, hackathons and multilingual learning platforms to promote app-based innovations.

Target Beneficiaries:


  • Undergraduate and postgraduate students pursuing careers in app development.
  • School, ITI, polytechnic and engineering college students seeking exposure to mobile technologies.
  • Startups, small scale industries and entrepreneurs in need of app development consultancy, UI/UX design expertise and testing facilities.
  • Government departments and local businesses aiming to digitalize their services via mobile platforms.

The CoE strives to bridge the gap between academia and industry, enhance employability skills, empower underprivileged communities and support India’s vision of a Digital First economy. With creativity, innovation and entrepreneurship at its core, it contributes to Atmanirbhar Bharat and enhances global digital empowerment.


  1. Advanced Mobile Development Lab:
    Equipped with high-end devices, simulators, testing tools and cloud integration platforms for Android, iOS and cross-platform development.
  2. Core Skills Training Modules:
    Hands-on facilities supporting the following domains:
    • Android Development: Kotlin, Java, and Jetpack frameworks.
    • iOS Development: Swift, Objective-C and Apple’s ecosystem.
    • Cross-Platform Development: Flutter, React Native and Xamarin.
    • UI/UX Design: Tools like Figma, Adobe XD and usability testing environments.
    • App Security & Performance: Mobile data security, secure coding and performance optimization.
  3. Smart Classrooms:
    Digitally enabled classrooms with AR/VR support, video conferencing and multilingual delivery for inclusive learning.
  4. Research & Innovation Hub:
    Infrastructure for scholars to explore emerging areas like AI-driven apps, IoT integration, AR/VR applications and m-health solutions.
  5. Knowledge Repository:
    Access to curated resources, frameworks, toolkits and industry case studies for continuous learning.
  1. Comprehensive Mobile App Development Courses:
    Covering fundamentals to advanced modules in Android, iOS, and cross-platform development.
    Includes live projects, debugging sessions, and app deployment to Google Play and Apple App Store.
  2. Real-time Industry Scenario Simulations:
    Hands-on exposure to challenges like handling large-scale user traffic, integrating payment gateways, and ensuring app security.
  3. Workshops for Minority & Underprivileged Students:
    Focused, free-of-cost programs to build employability through mobile app skills.
  4. Summer Schools & Internship Programs:
    Special modules for ITI and polytechnic students, emphasizing experiential learning with real-world projects.

    5. Additional programs are under design, including mobile game development, IoT-enabled apps and AI driven mobile solutions.
