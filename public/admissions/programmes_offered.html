B.Tech (Minor Degree) Programmes

In addition to above mentioned B.Tech programme, the institute offers its meritorious students to earn a minor degree in another engineering discipline in addition to Major degree in the which the student is originally enrolled. The students have to study six additional specialized courses (One in each semester starting from 3rd semester) in the engineering discipline of minor degree. Presently, the following combinations of Major and Minor degrees are operational:
