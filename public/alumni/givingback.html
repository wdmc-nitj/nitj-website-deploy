The NITJ Alumni are encouraged to donate under the following heads :


This fund is setup to raise donations for modernizing Hostels of NITJ. The funds will be used for renovating the common areas, bathrooms, mess hall, and rooms. The donations will help to make NITJ a world-class Institution of higher learning and provide the NITJians a modern place to call home during their time here.
The donors for the hostel infrastructure shall be acknowledged as follows:

Sr.No. Contribution Recognition
1 Funding for Construction of Hostel Room Nameplate in front of the room
2 Renovation/Maintenance of hostel rooms, common areas, bathrooms, mess hall, and hostel ground. Display of name-list in the hostel lobby

This fund is setup to raise donations for developing/strengthening the academic and research infrastructure of the Department. Considering the increasing expenses on academic and research activities at department level, the Institute plans to utilize these funds for overall growth and development of the department's infrastructure.

This fund is setup to raise donations for Construction/Renovation of Institute central facilities such as Institute Dispensary, Library, Institute Guest House, Institute computer centre Engineering workshop, Canteens and others. This fund will also be utilized to purchase e-rickshaw/Institute bus for campus residents and visitors.

Sr.No. Contribution Recognition
1 Funding for Construction of a new central facility e.g., Computer centre The new central facility will be named after the name of the donor
2 Funding for Construction of a certain portion of a new central facility (greater than INR 5,00,000/-) Nameplate in front of the constructed portion
3 Funding for Renovation/maintenance of existing
central facility (greater than INR 50,000/-)		 Display of name-list inside the facility

This fund is setup to raise donations for promoting the excellent academic environment and for the development of faculty members. This fund can be used to attract the excellent and promising faculty members by providing various fellowships and research funding. This fund will also cover regular industrial visits by NITJ faculty in order to enhance the industrial consultancy, R&D funding, patent-filing, internship & placement opportunities for the students, and to organize STCs/Workshops/Conferences/Expert Lectures.

This fund is setup to raise donations for supporting the nearby society of NITJ. Specific groups comprising of campus residents and students can be formed to organize the educational, health-related and environmental awareness programs (e.g. PRAYAAS Foundation of NITJ).

This fund is setup to raise donations for providing the travel support and cash awards to the students. The students will be encouraged to present their research work at National /International conferences. The students will be awarded cash prizes for publishing their work in top reputed International Journals.
