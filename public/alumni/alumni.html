NITJ ALUMNI ASSOCIATION (NITJAA)

NITJAA provides platform for students to interact with well settled alumni's of NITJ. It aids in gaining career advice and gives them direction towards better future by organising annual alumni meet. It renders students with career guidance and tailoring them for their future endeavours. Catering to the betterment of alumni-student relationship of Institute. To furnish alumni with a platform to interact with the students on a one to one basis and connect them with the Institute and provide career advice to prepare for the real world.

Vision: "To build a rich intellectual potential embedded with interdisciplinary knowledge, human values and professional ethics among the youth, aspirant of becoming engineers and technologists, so that they contribute to society and create a niche for a successful career".

Mission: "To become a leading and unique institution of higher learning, offering state-of-the- art education, research and training in engineering and technology to students who are able and eager to become change agents for the industrial and economic progress of the nation. To nurture and sustain an academic ambience conducive to the development and growth of committed professionals for sustainable development of the nation and to accomplish its integration into the global economy. To strive to be a Swachch NIT providing a guarantee of clean hostel, clean mess, clean water and hygienic & wholesome food to its students".

Mr. Amit Kumar Dixit, NITJ Alumnus has achieved the remarkable feat of securing the 627th rank in the UPSC CSE 2024

We are delighted to celebrate the outstanding achievement of Mr. Amit Kumar Dixit, an alumnus of Dr B R Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar, who has secured All India Rank 627 in the UPSC Civil Services Examination 2024. 


Hailing from Raebareli, Uttar Pradesh, Amit graduated with a B.Tech in Civil Engineering (Batch of 2014–2018). His journey to success was greatly supported by the unwavering encouragement of his mother, a homemaker, and the dedicated mentorship of his professors at NIT Jalandhar.


Amit’s passion for education was evident early on, as he began coaching JEE aspirants during his college years. Amid the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, he embarked on his UPSC journey while simultaneously managing a professional job. This was his third attempt, with Physics as his optional subject—a testament to his perseverance, especially after not clearing the prelims in his first two attempts.


Beyond academics, Amit was an active participant in campus life. He displayed his creativity and leadership by joining the Baware theatre club and contributing to the event management of technical festivals like TechNiti, fostering his holistic growth.


On behalf of the institute, Prof. B.K. Kanaujia, Director, NIT Jalandhar, extended his heartfelt congratulations to Amit on this remarkable milestone.


We are proud of Amit's accomplishment and wish him continued success and impact in his journey as a civil servant.

Ms. Sadaf Malik, NITJ Alumnus has achieved the remarkable feat of securing the 742nd rank in the UPSC CSE 2024

Sadaf Malik, a distinguished engineer from NIT Jalandhar, has captured national attention by excelling in the UPSC CSE 2024. Originating from Kapurthala, Punjab, her journey stands as a powerful example of determination and perseverance. 


Showing academic brilliance from the beginning, Sadaf pursued her B.Tech in Chemical Engineering at NIT Jalandhar (2016–2020 Batch). She began her professional career as a Data Analyst at ICICI Bank, Gurugram. However, driven by a deep aspiration to serve the nation, she courageously resigned from her job and devoted herself entirely to the demanding preparation for the UPSC examinations.


Her unwavering dedication paid off when she secured an impressive 742nd rank in the UPSC CSE 2024. With a strong focus on self-study and persistent effort, Sadaf’s determined pursuit of her dream has led her to the esteemed Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration (LBSNAA) in Mussoorie for her training.


Sadaf Malik’s inspiring journey of resilience and success continues to be a source of motivation for countless UPSC aspirants across the country.


Prof. Binod Kumar Kanaujia, Director of NIT Jalandhar, congratulations to Sadaf Malik on her outstanding accomplishment, noting that her success will inspire many other students to strive for excellence.

Manpreet Sharma, NITJ Alumnus achieves All India Rank 83 in UPSC

Manpreet Sharma, an alumnus of NIT Jalandhar’s 2016 Mechanical Engineering batch, has demonstrated Exceptional Academic and Professional Achievements. Hailing from the village of Lopon in the Moga District of Punjab, he comes from a family deeply dedicated to Public Service. His Mother is an Economics Lecturer at the Government Higher Secondary School in Moga, and his Father has dedicated nearly three decades to the field of Technical Education. His sister, Dr. Suman Sharma, is currently pursuing Postdoctoral Research in Horticulture at the University of Florence, USA, after completing her PhD at New Mexico State University, USA.
 

During his time at NIT Jalandhar, Manpreet ranked as the 4th topper of his batch. He displayed remarkable passion in his final-year project on the topic “Design and Fabrication of a Solar Thermal Storage Device using Phase Change Material,” under the guidance of Dr. Subash Chander. He also gained valuable Industry exposure through his summer internship at Tata Steel, Jamshedpur.


Manpreet began his professional career as an Assistant Manager at Maruti Suzuki India, where he served for three years. Driven by his aspiration to contribute to public administration, he later resigned to prepare for the Civil Services Examination. During this period, he actively engaged in social work through initiatives like ‘Mera Pind 360’ and founded ‘Mera Farmhouse,’ an agritech startup aimed at promoting rural development.


His perseverance paid off when he successfully cleared the UPSC Civil Services Examination, achieving an All India Rank of 83 in the reserve list of 2023, which was released on 25th October 2024.


Prof. Binod Kumar Kanaujia, Director of NIT Jalandhar, extends his Heartfelt Congratulations to Manpreet Sharma for his Outstanding Achievements and conveys his best wishes for Manpreet's Future Endeavors. His success is a source of great pride for the Institute, and we look forward to seeing his continued excellence in his journey of Service and Leadership.

An MoU signed between NIT Jalandhar and KISCO India Private Limited (KIPL)

The MoU was signed by Prof. Binod Kumar Kanaujia, Director of NIT Jalandhar, and Mr. Rohit Khanna, Managing Director of KIPL. The main objective of this collaboration is to extend vital financial support to academically gifted yet economically challenged students at NIT Jalandhar.
