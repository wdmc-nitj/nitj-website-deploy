About us

The program is led by Student Coordinators, and I-SAMP and the D-SAMP Faculty Coordinators. The responsible senior students to act as mentors for the junior students and address any problems (academic as well as non-academic) they might be having at the department as well as at the Institute level. It is a fairly simple yet effective framework for helping students and enabling positive discussions with seniors. It is also quite flexible and leaves room for many activities - Social Service Endeavours, Team meetups, Mentor-Mentee treats. The different departments implement this idea in vastly different manners to suit their needs and maximize student wellness. Here, I-SAMP and D-SAMP are Institute and Department level coordinators,respectively.

Mentor Section

The responsible senior students to act as mentors for the junior students and address any problems (academic as well as non-academic) they might be having at the department as well as at the Institute level.

Mentee Section

Whether it's issues within the department or broader concerns at the institute level, mentees can rely on their mentors for assistance and advice, creating a nurturing environment for personal and academic growth
